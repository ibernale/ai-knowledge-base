# Our evaluation of OpenAI's GPT-5.5 cyber capabilities

## Why it matters
The UK AISI has evaluated GPT-5.5's offensive cyber capabilities and found it reaches similar levels to Anthropic's Claude Mythos Preview. GPT-5.5 achieved 71.4% on Expert-tier cyber tasks (vs Mythos's 68.6%) and is the second model to complete the 32-step 'The Last Ones' corporate network attack simulation end-to-end (2/10 attempts vs Mythos's 3/10). This suggests offensive cyber capability is emerging as a frontier-wide shift rather than a single-lab breakthrough. AISI also found a universal jailbreak in 6 hours of red-teaming. The evaluation confirms cyber offense scales with general reasoning capabilities.

## Lede (original)
AISI conducted cyber evaluations on OpenAI's GPT-5.5. GPT-5.5 is one of the strongest models we have tested on our cyber tasks and is the second model to solve one of our multi-step cyber-attack simulations end-to-end. Our advanced suite tasks are specifically designed to probe the capabilities we consider most important to measure, focusing on vulnerability research and exploitation against realistic targets with modern mitigations.
