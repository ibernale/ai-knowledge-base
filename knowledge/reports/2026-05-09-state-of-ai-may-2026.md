# State of AI: May 2026

## Why it matters
Air Street's monthly State of AI covers three major developments: (1) Two frontier models—Claude Mythos Preview and GPT-5.5—cleared AISI's 32-step end-to-end cyber-attack range within a month, with UK AISI estimating frontier cyber-offense capability doubles every four months; (2) Cohere and Aleph Alpha merged as a 'sovereign AI' alternative to US-China duopoly; (3) China formally blocked Meta's $2B acquisition of agent startup Manus—the first state-level prohibition of an inbound AI acquisition. Essential monthly signal aggregation for tracking geopolitical and capability dynamics.

## Lede (original)
Two frontier models cleared a 32-step end-to-end cyber-attack range in a single month. Anthropic's Claude Mythos Preview did it first; OpenAI's GPT-5.5 followed three weeks later. The UK's AI Security Institute now estimates frontier cyber-offence capability is doubling every four months.
