# The 2026 AI Index Report

## Why it matters
The definitive annual snapshot of AI's state. Key findings: SWE-bench coding performance jumped from 60% to near 100% in one year; organizational adoption hit 88%; US-China lead has effectively closed (Anthropic at 1,503 Elo leads by just 2.7%); Foundation Model Transparency Index dropped from 58 to 40 as top models disclose less. Major perception gap: 73% of US AI experts see positive job market impact vs. 23% of general public. US AI researcher immigration collapsed 89% since 2017, with 80% of that in the last year alone.

## Lede (original)
This year's Index reveals a widening gap between what AI can do and how prepared we are to manage it. While AI continues its rapid integration into the global economy – with technical capabilities improving, investment accelerating, and adoption spreading – the frameworks needed to govern, evaluate, and understand this technology are falling behind.
