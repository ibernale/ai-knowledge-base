# Microsoft New Future of Work Report 2025

## Why it matters
Microsoft Research's annual Future of Work report shifts focus from individual productivity to collective productivity—how teams and organizations can benefit from AI together. Key findings: AI is mediating decisions and relationships across work, education, and civic spheres; workers resist top-down AI mandates that prioritize efficiency over quality; and non-linear productivity gains emerge when AI improvements trigger discrete reorganizations of work. For leaders implementing AI, the report provides research-backed guidance on adoption patterns, team dynamics, and the psychological impacts of AI integration.

## Lede (original)
Last year's report highlighted research showing that AI delivers substantial gains in individual productivity. The next frontier, covered in this year's report, is collective productivity: how teams, organizations, and communities can get better together. AI can bridge gaps of time, distance, and scale, but only if built correctly.
