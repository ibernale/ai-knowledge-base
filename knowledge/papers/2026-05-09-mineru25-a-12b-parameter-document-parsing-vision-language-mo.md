# MinerU2.5: A 1.2B-Parameter Document Parsing Vision-Language Model

## Why it matters
MinerU2.5 is a 1.2B-parameter vision-language model for document parsing that achieves state-of-the-art recognition accuracy through a coarse-to-fine parsing strategy. The model matches much larger VLMs on document understanding tasks while being efficient enough for production deployment. This is relevant for practitioners building document processing pipelines who need the quality of frontier multimodal models without the inference costs.

## Abstract (original)
