# UniVidX: A Unified Multimodal Framework for Video Generation

## Why it matters
UniVidX presents a unified multimodal framework that uses video diffusion model priors for versatile video generation through stochastic condition masking, decoupled gated LoRA, and cross-modal self-attention mechanisms.
