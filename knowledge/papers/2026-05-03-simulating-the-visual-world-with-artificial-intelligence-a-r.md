# Simulating the Visual World with Artificial Intelligence: A Roadmap

## Why it matters
This comprehensive roadmap frames modern video foundation models as implicit world models combined with video renderers. It proposes a four-generation taxonomy: faithfulness, interactiveness, planning, and stochasticity—representing the progression from visual generation to genuine reality simulation. The analysis is particularly valuable for robotics and autonomous driving applications, stress-testing frontier models to reveal limitations in spatial reasoning and causal understanding. It provides clear milestones for evaluating progress toward agentic world models that can support embodied AI.

## Abstract (original)
Video generation is evolving from merely creating visually appealing clips to constructing virtual environments that obey physical laws and enable real-time interaction. We conceptualize modern video foundation models as the fusion of two key components: an implicit world model that simulates physics and causal dynamics, and a video renderer that visualizes these internal simulations.
