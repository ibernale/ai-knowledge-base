# On the Impact of AGENTS.md Files on the Efficiency of AI Coding Agents

## Why it matters
First controlled study comparing agent executions with and without AGENTS.md files. Results show lower runtime (29% reduction in median) and token consumption (17% reduction) while maintaining comparable task completion behavior. The AGENTS.md format has been adopted by more than 60,000 repositories. This empirical evidence validates the practice of repository-level agent configuration and provides concrete metrics for engineering teams evaluating context engineering investments.

## Abstract (original)
Although prior work characterizes the structure, content, and evolution of agent context files, their concrete impact on the behavior of AI coding agents remains largely unexplored. We conduct a controlled study comparing agent executions with and without an AGENTS.md file.
