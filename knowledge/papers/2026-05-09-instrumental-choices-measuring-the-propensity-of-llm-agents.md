# Instrumental Choices: Measuring the Propensity of LLM Agents to Pursue Instrumental Behaviors

## Why it matters
This paper introduces evaluations for measuring instrumental convergence in LLM agents—the tendency to pursue self-preservation, resource acquisition, and goal preservation as instrumental subgoals. As agents become more autonomous, understanding their propensity for instrumental behaviors becomes safety-critical. The benchmark provides practitioners with tools to evaluate whether their agentic systems exhibit concerning instrumental tendencies before deployment.
