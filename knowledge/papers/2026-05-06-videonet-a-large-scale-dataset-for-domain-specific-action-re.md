# VideoNet: A Large-Scale Dataset for Domain-Specific Action Recognition

## Why it matters
A CVPR 2026 Highlight paper from a team including Yejin Choi, Ali Farhadi, and Hannaneh Hajishirzi at UW/AI2. VideoNet provides a large-scale dataset for domain-specific action recognition, addressing the gap between general video understanding and specialized domains. The combination of senior researchers and CVPR Highlight status signals this will become a standard benchmark for video foundation models doing action recognition in specific verticals.
