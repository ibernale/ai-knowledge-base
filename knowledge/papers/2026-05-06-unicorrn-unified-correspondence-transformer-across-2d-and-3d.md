# UniCorrn: Unified Correspondence Transformer Across 2D and 3D

## Why it matters
A unified correspondence transformer that works across 2D and 3D, addressing a fundamental computer vision problem. Correspondence matching is foundational for SLAM, 3D reconstruction, and robotics applications. A unified architecture that handles both 2D and 3D reduces the complexity of vision pipelines and enables better transfer between domains.
