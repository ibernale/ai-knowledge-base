# HERMES++: Toward a Unified Driving World Model for 3D Scene Understanding and Generation

## Why it matters
HERMES++ introduces a unified driving world model integrating 3D scene understanding with future geometry prediction via shared Bird's-Eye View representation and LLM-enhanced world queries. Achieves 41.6% reduction in Chamfer Distance for future point cloud generation and 2.3% CIDEr gain for scene understanding. Extended version of ICCV 2025 paper with code available. Relevant for robotics and autonomous vehicle practitioners building predictive world models.

## Abstract (original)
HERMES++ introduces a unified driving world model that integrates 3D scene understanding with future geometry prediction by leveraging a shared Bird's-Eye View representation and LLM-enhanced world queries. The model achieved a 41.6% reduction in Chamfer Distance for future point cloud generation and a 2.3% gain in CIDEr for scene understanding over previous specialist approaches.
