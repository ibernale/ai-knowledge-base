# ReVSI: Revised Benchmark for Video Spatial Intelligence

## Why it matters
Addresses flaws in current spatial intelligence evaluation for video models by creating a validated benchmark with improved annotations and controlled frame sampling conditions. As multimodal models increasingly claim spatial reasoning capabilities, rigorous benchmarks become essential for honest progress tracking. Part of broader trend toward more careful evaluation methodology.
