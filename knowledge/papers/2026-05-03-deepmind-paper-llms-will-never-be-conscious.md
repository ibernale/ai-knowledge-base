# DeepMind Paper: LLMs Will Never Be Conscious

## Why it matters
A senior DeepMind staff scientist argues in a new paper that no AI or computational system will ever become conscious. This directly contradicts the AGI narratives from AI lab CEOs including DeepMind's own Demis Hassabis, who claims AGI will have '10 times the impact of the Industrial Revolution.' The paper's existence within DeepMind highlights internal disagreement about AI capabilities and what 'intelligence' actually means. For practitioners, this provides grounding against hype while raising questions about how frontier labs internally debate these fundamental questions.

## Abstract (original)
A senior staff scientist at Google's artificial intelligence laboratory DeepMind, Alexander Lerchner, argues in a new paper that no AI or other computational system will ever become conscious. That conclusion appears to conflict with the narrative from AI company CEOs, including DeepMind's own Demis Hassabis, who repeatedly talks about the advent of artificial general intelligence.
