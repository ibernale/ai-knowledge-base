# VibeVoice: Long-Form Multi-Speaker Speech Synthesis via Next-Token Diffusion

## Why it matters
VibeVoice synthesizes long-form multi-speaker speech using next-token diffusion and a highly efficient continuous speech tokenizer. The approach achieves superior performance and fidelity compared to prior work, enabling more natural podcast-style and multi-character audio generation. Relevant for teams building audio applications, voice agents, or content generation tools where multi-speaker synthesis quality matters.
