# Stop Automating Peer Review Without Rigorous Evaluation

## Why it matters
Accepted as an ICML 2026 Spotlight, this position paper argues against deploying AI systems for peer review without rigorous evaluation. As conferences increasingly experiment with AI-assisted review, this work establishes methodological requirements for validating such systems. The spotlight designation indicates the ML community considers this an important intervention in ongoing debates about AI in scientific publishing.

## Abstract (original)
We argue that automating peer review without rigorous evaluation poses significant risks to scientific quality and integrity. We propose evaluation frameworks that any AI peer review system should meet before deployment.
