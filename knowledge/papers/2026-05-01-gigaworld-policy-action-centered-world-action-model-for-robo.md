# GigaWorld-Policy: Action-Centered World-Action Model for Robotic Policy Learning

## Why it matters
Introduces an action-centered World-Action Model that improves robotic policy learning by decoupling visual and motion representations. Enables faster inference and better task performance through dual supervision from action prediction and video generation. Addresses a key bottleneck in robot learning: current approaches either sacrifice visual fidelity for action precision or vice versa. GigaWorld-Policy achieves both, making it immediately relevant for production robotics systems.
