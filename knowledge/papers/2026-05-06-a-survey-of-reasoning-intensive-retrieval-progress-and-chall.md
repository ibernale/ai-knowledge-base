# A Survey of Reasoning-Intensive Retrieval: Progress and Challenges

## Why it matters
Accepted to ACL 2026 Main Conference. This survey covers reasoning-intensive retrieval—the intersection of RAG and complex reasoning that's becoming critical as agents need to retrieve information and reason about it simultaneously. For practitioners building agentic RAG systems, this provides the taxonomy and benchmarking framework for the space.
