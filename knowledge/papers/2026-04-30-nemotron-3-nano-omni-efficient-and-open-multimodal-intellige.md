# Nemotron 3 Nano Omni: Efficient and Open Multimodal Intelligence

## Why it matters
NVIDIA's Nemotron 3 Nano Omni unifies video, audio, image, and text understanding into a single 30B-A3B hybrid MoE model, achieving up to 9x higher throughput than competing open omni models. The model uses Conv3D-based temporal video compression and Efficient Video Sampling to dramatically reduce compute costs. This matters for agentic AI because it eliminates the fragmented model chains that lose time and context when passing data between separate vision, audio, and language models. Already adopted by Palantir, Foxconn, and H Company for production agents.

## Abstract (original)
We introduced Nemotron 3 Nano Omni, an efficient omni-modal model that extends the Nemotron multimodal family with native audio support and consistently stronger reasoning across text, images, video, and audio. The model achieves leading results in document understanding, audio-visual reasoning, and audio benchmarks.
