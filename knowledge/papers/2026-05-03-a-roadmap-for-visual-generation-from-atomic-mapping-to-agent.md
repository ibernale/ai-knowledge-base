# A Roadmap for Visual Generation: From Atomic Mapping to Agentic World Modeling

## Why it matters
Presents a five-level taxonomy for visual generation evolution from atomic mapping to agentic world modeling. Analyzes current generative paradigms and training advancements, stress-tests frontier models to reveal limitations in spatial reasoning and causal understanding. Provides research directions for the next generation of visual AI systems.

## Abstract (original)
A roadmap outlines the evolution of visual generation, presenting a five-level taxonomy from atomic mapping to agentic world modeling. It analyzes current generative paradigms, training advancements, and evaluates frontier models, revealing limitations in spatial reasoning and causal understanding.
