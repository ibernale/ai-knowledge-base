# The Last Human-Written Paper: Agent-Native Research Artifacts

## Why it matters
Proposes Agent-Native Research Artifact (ARA), a protocol that reframes research contributions as machine-executable knowledge packages rather than traditional papers. Quantifiably improves knowledge extraction by 21.3%, boosts reproduction success by 7.0%, and accelerates early-phase research extensions for AI agents. A direct challenge to the PDF-centric publishing paradigm—argues that as AI agents become primary research consumers, artifacts should be structured for machine rather than human comprehension.

## Abstract (original)
A protocol called Agent-Native Research Artifact (ARA) is introduced, reframing research contributions as machine-executable knowledge packages. This framework quantifiably improves knowledge extraction by 21.3%, boosts reproduction success by 7.0%, and accelerates early-phase research extensions for AI agents.
