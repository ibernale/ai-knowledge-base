# My bets on open models, mid-2026

## Why it matters
Nathan Lambert from AI2 provides an insider's analysis of open models' competitive position. He argues the obvious answer is that open models won't keep pace with closed labs in every area, but the nuance is in understanding which areas matter. His analysis of funding dynamics, distillation effectiveness across domains, and regulatory considerations offers practitioners a framework for deciding when open models are sufficient versus when frontier access is necessary.

## Lede (original)
We're living through the period of time when we'll learn if open models can keep up with closed labs. The obvious answer is that no, they won't. This answer is a form of saying they won't keep up in every area. This framing closes off a popular prediction where the open models completely catch up.
