# AI for Science: Training Transformers to solve 95% failure rate of Cancer Trials

## Why it matters
Latent Space interviews Noetik's founders on applying transformers to cancer trial matching—a domain where 95% of treatments fail clinical trials partly due to a matching problem. If we better understood which patients have which tumors that will respond to which treatments, we could dramatically improve outcomes. This represents a concrete application of frontier AI to high-stakes medical decision-making, with immediate implications for healthcare AI deployment.

## Lede (original)
95% of cancer treatments fail to pass clinical trials, but it may be a matching problem—if we better understood what patients have which tumors which will respond to which treatment.
