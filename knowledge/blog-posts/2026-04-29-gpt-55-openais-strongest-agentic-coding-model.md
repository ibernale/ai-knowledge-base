# GPT-5.5: OpenAI's strongest agentic coding model

## Why it matters
GPT-5.5 represents OpenAI's push toward a unified 'super app' combining ChatGPT, Codex, and browser capabilities. Achieves 82.7% on Terminal-Bench 2.0 for complex command-line workflows and state-of-the-art on SWE-Bench Pro. OpenAI claims it delivers frontier intelligence at half the cost of competitive models on Artificial Analysis Coding Index. Available to Plus, Pro, Business, Enterprise users in ChatGPT and Codex; API access coming soon. Key differentiator is efficiency—fewer tokens and retries to reach quality outputs.

## Lede (original)
GPT‑5.5 understands what you're trying to do faster and can carry more of the work itself. It excels at writing and debugging code, researching online, analyzing data, creating documents and spreadsheets, operating software, and moving across tools until a task is finished. Instead of carefully managing every step, you can give GPT‑5.5 a messy, multi-part task and trust it to plan, use tools, check its work, navigate through ambiguity, and keep going.
