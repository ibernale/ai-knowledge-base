# Pentagon strikes deals with 7 Big Tech companies after shunning Anthropic

## Why it matters
The Pentagon signed agreements with seven major tech companies (OpenAI, Google, Microsoft, Nvidia, AWS, SpaceX, Reflection AI) for AI deployment in classified networks at IL6/IL7 Top Secret levels—notably excluding Anthropic. This exclusion stems from Anthropic's insistence on safety guardrails for military use. The news signals the divergence between commercial AI deployment and safety-first approaches, with implications for how AI governance frameworks will evolve. The White House is reportedly reconsidering its position after Anthropic's Mythos capabilities proved too significant to ignore.

## Lede (original)
The Department of Defense announced Friday an agreement with seven major technology companies to use their artificial intelligence tools in its classified networks. Not included: Anthropic, which the Trump administration has blacklisted over Anthropic's insistence that the Pentagon include certain safety guardrails for the government's use of AI in warfare.
