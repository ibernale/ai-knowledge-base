# The people do not yearn for automation

## Why it matters
Simon Willison amplifies Nilay Patel's essay exploring the paradox of AI's unpopularity with the general public despite skyrocketing ChatGPT usage. This analysis is crucial for practitioners building AI products: understanding user sentiment versus behavior gaps helps explain adoption friction and informs product design choices. The piece examines why automation faces cultural resistance even as capabilities improve.
