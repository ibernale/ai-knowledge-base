# Anthropic partners with SpaceX for orbital AI compute capacity

## Why it matters
Anthropic announces a partnership with SpaceX for compute capacity, including interest in developing 'multiple gigawatts of orbital AI compute capacity.' This signals frontier labs exploring unconventional compute infrastructure as demand outstrips terrestrial data center growth. Anthropic also lifts five-hour rate caps for most paid subscribers. The orbital compute ambition, while speculative, indicates the scale of compute infrastructure discussions at frontier labs.

## Lede (original)
In addition to the partnership with SpaceX, Anthropic said it will lift the five-hour rate caps for most paid subscribers including those on its Pro, Max, Team and Enterprise plans. As part of this agreement, Anthropic also expressed interest in partnering to develop multiple gigawatts of orbital AI compute capacity.
