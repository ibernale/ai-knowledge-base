# Deep Research Max: a step change for autonomous research agents

## Why it matters
Google released two autonomous research agents built on Gemini 3.1 Pro: Deep Research (optimized for speed) and Deep Research Max (maximum comprehensiveness). Key advances include MCP support for connecting to private data sources, native chart/infographic generation, and collaborative plan editing. Deep Research Max achieves 93.3% on DeepSearchQA (up from 66.1%) and 54.6% on Humanity's Last Exam. This positions Google to compete directly in the enterprise research agent market against OpenAI and Anthropic.

## Lede (original)
Built with Gemini 3.1 Pro, the new Deep Research agents bring MCP support, native visualizations and unprecedented analytical quality to long-horizon research workflows across the web or custom sources. In December, we released the Gemini Deep Research agent to developers via the Interactions API, giving developers access to Google's most advanced autonomous research capabilities. Today, we are taking these capabilities to the next level with two new evolutions of our autonomous research agent: Deep Research and Deep Research Max.
