# Testing ads in ChatGPT

## Why it matters
OpenAI begins rolling out ads for Free and Go plan users in Australia, New Zealand, and Canada—a significant business model shift. Plus, Pro, Business, Enterprise, and Education plans will not have ads. The company frames this as helping users 'discover relevant products and services' as they 'explore what they may need, evaluate options, and make decisions.' This marks OpenAI's first move into advertising-supported AI assistants.

## Lede (original)
As people turn to ChatGPT to explore what they may need, evaluate options, and make decisions, we see an exciting opportunity to help them discover relevant products and services through ads.
