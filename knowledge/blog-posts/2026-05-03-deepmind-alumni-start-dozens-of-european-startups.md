# DeepMind Alumni Start Dozens of European Startups

## Why it matters
Data shows 112 DeepMind alumni have founded or plan to launch startups in the last 18 months, with David Silver's Ineffable Intelligence raising a $1.1B seed round. Of 112 new startups, 70 are US-based, 28 in the UK. Notable names include Noah Goodman (Humans&, $480M seed), Olivier Henaff (Cursive, backed by Sovereign AI fund), and Saining Xie (diffusion research at NYU). This 'founder factory' effect mirrors what Klarna and Spotify did for European tech, representing compounding talent distribution across the AI ecosystem.

## Lede (original)
Google DeepMind alumni have founded dozens of European startups or plan to launch startups in the past 18 months, new data shows. Ex-DeepMind luminary David Silver captured headlines last month when his new UK AI lab Ineffable Intelligence landed a massive $1.1bn seed funding round.
