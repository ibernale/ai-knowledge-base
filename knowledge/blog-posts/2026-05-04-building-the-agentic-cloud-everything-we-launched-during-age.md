# Building the agentic cloud: everything we launched during Agents Week 2026

## Why it matters
Cloudflare's Agents Week 2026 wrap-up covers ~30 announcements establishing infrastructure for production agentic workloads: Sandboxes GA (persistent compute per agent), Workflows V2 (50K concurrent instances, up from 4.5K), Dynamic Workers (100x faster sandboxing), Agent Memory, versioned storage via Git, and unified AI Platform. The positioning is 'if knowledge workers each run a few agents in parallel, you need compute for tens of millions of simultaneous sessions'—a clear infrastructure play for the agentic era.

## Lede (original)
Today marks the end of our first Agents Week, an innovation week dedicated entirely to the age of agents. Over the past year, agents have swiftly changed how people work. Coding agents are helping developers ship faster than ever. Support agents resolve tickets end-to-end. Research agents validate hypotheses across hundreds of sources in minutes.
