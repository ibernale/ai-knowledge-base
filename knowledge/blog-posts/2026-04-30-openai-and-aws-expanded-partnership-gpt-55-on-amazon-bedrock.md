# OpenAI and AWS Expanded Partnership: GPT-5.5 on Amazon Bedrock

## Why it matters
OpenAI and AWS launched GPT-5.5 and other frontier models on Amazon Bedrock, along with Codex on Bedrock and Amazon Bedrock Managed Agents powered by OpenAI. This represents a major shift in OpenAI's distribution strategy following the renegotiated Microsoft partnership. Enterprises can now access OpenAI's frontier models through AWS's infrastructure, security controls, and existing procurement workflows. More than 4 million people now use Codex weekly.

## Lede (original)
OpenAI launches GPT-5.5 and other frontier models on Amazon Bedrock, brings Codex to AWS, and adds Bedrock Managed Agents powered by OpenAI, giving enterprises new ways to build and deploy AI in trusted AWS environments.
