# AIE Europe Debrief + Agent Labs Thesis: Latent Space x Unsupervised Learning Crossover

## Why it matters
This crossover episode with Unsupervised Learning captures the state of AI coding markets in April 2026: Anthropic at ~$2.5B ARR from Claude Code, OpenAI at ~$2B, Cursor at ~$2B. Swyx's thesis that '2025 was the year of coding agents, 2026 is coding agents breaking containment to do everything else' provides a clear framing for where the industry is headed. The discussion covers why coding AI has stickier product dynamics than consumer AI, and where application companies can still compete against foundation model providers. Essential listening for anyone building AI products.
