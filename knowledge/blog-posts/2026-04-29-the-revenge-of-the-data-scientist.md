# The Revenge of the Data Scientist

## Why it matters
Hamel Husain argues that the 'harness engineering' paradigm—where agents work autonomously within test/spec/metric boundaries—fundamentally requires data science skills. Drawing on OpenAI's Codex harness engineering blog and Karpathy's auto-research work, he makes the case that metrics, observability, and eval design are the critical constraints that make agentic systems work. For teams building agentic products, this reframes hiring and skill development priorities.

## Lede (original)
Is the heyday of the data scientist over? The Harvard Business Review once called it 'The Sexiest Job of the 21st Century.' For years, shipping AI meant keeping data scientists and MLEs on the critical path. With LLMs, this stopped being the default.
