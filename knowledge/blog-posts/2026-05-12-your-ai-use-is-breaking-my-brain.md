# Your AI Use Is Breaking My Brain

## Why it matters
Simon links to Jason Koebler's piece on how AI-generated content is becoming impossible to filter, causing mental exhaustion and distorting human writing. This 'zombie internet' problem—AI summaries sold as real books, AI-generated Reddit threads, marketing accounts posing as humans—represents an emerging challenge for information quality. Relevant for practitioners thinking about AI's second-order effects on trust and content authenticity online.

## Lede (original)
Excellent, angry piece by Jason Koebler on how AI writing online is becoming impossible to avoid, filtering it is mentally exhausting and it's even starting to distort regular human writing.
