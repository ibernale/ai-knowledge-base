# Gemini Robotics ER 1.6: Enhanced Embodied Reasoning

## Why it matters
Significant upgrade to DeepMind's reasoning-first robotics model enabling robots to understand environments with unprecedented precision. Positions embodied reasoning as a key capability for bridging the gap between language model intelligence and physical world manipulation. Part of DeepMind's broader push to make Gemini models the foundation for robotics applications, competing with specialized robotics foundation models.

## Lede (original)
Today, we're introducing Gemini Robotics-ER 1.6, a significant upgrade to our reasoning-first model that enables robots to understand their environments with unprecedented precision.
