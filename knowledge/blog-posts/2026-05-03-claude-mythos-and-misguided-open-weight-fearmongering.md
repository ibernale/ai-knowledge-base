# Claude Mythos and misguided open-weight fearmongering

## Why it matters
Lambert's response to Anthropic's Mythos Preview release argues that the cybersecurity capabilities discovered don't justify restricting open-weight releases—because the relevant capabilities will diffuse to open models within months anyway. He challenges the 'defense-only' framing, noting that asymmetric access to offensive capabilities may not actually favor defenders. This is an important counterpoint to the Project Glasswing narrative, and provides practitioners with a framework for thinking about capability diffusion timelines when making security decisions.

## Lede (original)
Another dance around fears of open-source. The arguments for restricting open models based on cybersecurity capabilities need to account for how quickly those capabilities diffuse to open-weight alternatives.
