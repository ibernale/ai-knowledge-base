# Learning on the Shop floor

## Why it matters
Simon highlights Tobias Lütke's description of Shopify's internal coding agent 'River,' which operates entirely in public Slack channels—refusing DMs and suggesting public channels instead. Lütke's channel has 100+ observers who react, add context, and learn by watching. This 'Lehrwerkstatt' (German for teaching workshop) model demonstrates a novel organizational pattern for AI-assisted development where transparency enables learning at scale. Relevant for engineering leaders designing team workflows around coding agents.

## Lede (original)
Tobias Lütke describes Shopify's internal coding agent tool, River, which operates entirely in public on their Slack: River does not respond to direct messages. She politely declines and suggests to create a public channel for you and her to start working in.
