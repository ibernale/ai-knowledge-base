# Scaling Trusted Access for Cyber with GPT-5.5 and GPT-5.5-Cyber

## Why it matters
OpenAI is rolling out GPT-5.5-Cyber in limited preview to vetted cybersecurity defenders, creating a tiered access framework that balances capability with safety. The model is trained to be more permissive on security-related tasks like vulnerability identification, malware analysis, and reverse engineering, while still blocking credential theft and malware deployment. UK AISI testing shows GPT-5.5 completed a 32-step corporate network attack in 2/10 runs, matching Anthropic's Mythos. Beginning June 1, 2026, individual users accessing the most permissive models will need phishing-resistant Advanced Account Security, treating cyber authorization as an access-control problem rather than just content moderation.

## Lede (original)
We are rolling out GPT-5.5-Cyber in limited preview to defenders responsible for securing critical infrastructure to support specialized cybersecurity workflows that help protect the broader ecosystem. We are focused on providing proportional safeguards and access to empower cyber defenders to protect society.
