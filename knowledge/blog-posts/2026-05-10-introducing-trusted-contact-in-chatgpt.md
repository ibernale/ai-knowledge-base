# Introducing Trusted Contact in ChatGPT

## Why it matters
OpenAI introduces a 'trusted contact' feature allowing users to designate someone who can be notified if automated systems and trained reviewers detect serious suicide-related safety concerns. Available for personal ChatGPT accounts (not Business/Enterprise/Edu), this represents a novel approach to AI safety interventions that bridges digital conversations with real-world support networks—a first for consumer AI assistants.

## Lede (original)
Trusted contact in ChatGPT helps you connect with a person you trust if you need additional support. You may choose your trusted contact in settings, and ChatGPT will send them an invitation to participate.
