# AWS What's Next 2026: OpenAI Partnership and Amazon Quick

## Why it matters
AWS announced Amazon Quick, an AI assistant that connects to work apps, learns preferences, and takes action autonomously. The event also detailed the OpenAI partnership: GPT-5.5 and GPT-5.4 on Bedrock, Codex on Bedrock, and Bedrock Managed Agents powered by OpenAI—all in limited preview. The Managed Agents feature is built with OpenAI's harness for faster execution, sharper reasoning, and reliable long-running task steering. This represents AWS's aggressive move into the agentic AI space with frontier model partnerships.

## Lede (original)
AWS and OpenAI are bringing the latest OpenAI models to Amazon Bedrock, launching Codex on Amazon Bedrock, and launching Amazon Bedrock Managed Agents, powered by OpenAI, giving enterprises the frontier intelligence they want on the infrastructure they trust. Amazon Quick is an AI assistant for work that connects to all of them, learns what matters to you, and takes action on your behalf.
