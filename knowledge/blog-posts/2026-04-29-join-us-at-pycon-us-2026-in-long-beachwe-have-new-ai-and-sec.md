# Join us at PyCon US 2026 in Long Beach—we have new AI and security tracks this year

## Why it matters
Simon Willison announces PyCon US 2026's new dedicated AI and Security tracks (May 13-19, Long Beach). The AI track was organized by Silona Bonewald (CitableAI) and Zac Hatfield-Dodds (Anthropic). For practitioners interested in the intersection of Python development and AI engineering, this represents a significant signal of AI's mainstreaming into the broader developer community.

## Lede (original)
This year's PyCon US is coming up next month from May 13th to May 19th, with the core conference talks from Friday 15th to Sunday 17th. In addition to regular PyCon programming we have two new dedicated tracks at the conference this year: an AI track on Friday and a Security track on Saturday.
