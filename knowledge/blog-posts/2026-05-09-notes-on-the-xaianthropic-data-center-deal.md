# Notes on the xAI/Anthropic data center deal

## Why it matters
Simon Willison analyzes Anthropic's partnership with SpaceX to access Colossus 1 datacenter (300+ megawatts, equivalent to 220,000+ NVIDIA GPUs). The deal addresses Anthropic's compute constraints amid 80x growth—Dario Amodei called the growth 'just crazy' and 'too hard to handle.' This represents an unusual cross-company compute arrangement and signals how infrastructure bottlenecks are forcing frontier labs into unexpected partnerships.
