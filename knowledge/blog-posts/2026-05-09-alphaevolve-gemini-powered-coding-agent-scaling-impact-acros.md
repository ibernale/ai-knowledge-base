# AlphaEvolve: Gemini-powered coding agent scaling impact across fields

## Why it matters
One year after AlphaEvolve's introduction, DeepMind reports substantial real-world impact: 30% reduction in DNA sequencing errors via DeepConsensus improvements, a jump from 14% to 88% feasible solutions in AC Optimal Power Flow problems, 5% improvement in natural disaster prediction accuracy across 20 categories, and 10x lower quantum circuit errors enabling new Willow processor experiments. This demonstrates how Gemini-powered evolutionary coding agents are moving beyond proof-of-concept to drive measurable gains across genomics, grid optimization, earth sciences, and quantum computing—validating the general-purpose algorithm discovery paradigm.

## Lede (original)
A year ago, we introduced AlphaEvolve, a Gemini-powered coding agent for designing advanced algorithms. We showed that AlphaEvolve can help make new discoveries on open problems across mathematics and computer science, and optimize algorithms that have since been deployed across critical parts of Google's infrastructure.
