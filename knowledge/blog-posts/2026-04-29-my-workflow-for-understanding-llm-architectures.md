# My Workflow for Understanding LLM Architectures

## Why it matters
Raschka provides a systematic learning-oriented workflow for practitioners trying to keep up with rapid open-weight model releases. Rather than just reading technical reports, he details how to extract architectural innovations from model releases like Gemma 4 by examining config files, comparing against known baselines, and building minimal implementations. Practical guide for AI engineers who need to quickly understand whether a new architecture contains innovations worth adopting.

## Lede (original)
A learning-oriented workflow for understanding new open-weight model releases. I put together a new LLM Architecture Gallery that collects the architecture figures from my recent comparison articles in one place, together with compact fact sheets and links.
