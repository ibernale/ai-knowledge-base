# Introducing Petri 3.0

## Why it matters
Meridian Labs' first release as Petri's new steward details the 3.0 architecture that decouples auditor and target models for independent customization. The UK AI Security Institute used a 3.0 prototype for pre-deployment evaluations of Claude Mythos and Opus 4.7. Constellation and Anthropic Fellows used it for independent safety evaluation of Kimi K2.5. The tool now sits alongside Inspect AI, Inspect Scout, and Inspect Flow in Meridian's open evaluation stack. This establishes Meridian as the neutral home for alignment evaluation infrastructure that multiple labs and governments can rely on.

## Lede (original)
Today we're releasing Petri 3.0. It's the biggest update to the alignment auditing agent yet, and the first developed here at Meridian—Petri's new home. In the seven months since its release, Petri has become the basis for a range of alignment research.
