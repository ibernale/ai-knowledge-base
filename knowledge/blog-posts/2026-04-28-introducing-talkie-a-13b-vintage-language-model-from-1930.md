# Introducing talkie: a 13B vintage language model from 1930

## Why it matters
A unique research project from Nick Levine, David Duvenaud, and Alec Radford (co-creator of GPT, GPT-2, Whisper). talkie-1930-13b-base is a 13B language model trained exclusively on 260B tokens of pre-1931 historical text. This is an interesting experiment in temporal distribution shift and could inform understanding of how training data composition affects model behavior and knowledge cutoffs.
