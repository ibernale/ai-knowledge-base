# OpenAI Releases ChatGPT for Clinicians

## Why it matters
OpenAI releases a free clinical AI version for verified US physicians, NPs, PAs, and pharmacists. Includes documentation support, medical research, trusted search, reusable workflows, CME support, and HIPAA options. Also introduces HealthBench Professional evaluation suite and Health Blueprint for safer healthcare AI. Represents OpenAI's vertical expansion strategy and serious engagement with regulated industries.

## Lede (original)
We're introducing ChatGPT for Clinicians, a version of ChatGPT designed to support clinical tasks like documentation and medical research so clinicians can focus on delivering high-quality patient care. We're making it free for any verified physician, NP, PA, or pharmacist, starting in the U.S.
