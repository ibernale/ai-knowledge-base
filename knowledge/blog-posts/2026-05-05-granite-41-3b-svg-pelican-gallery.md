# Granite 4.1 3B SVG Pelican Gallery

## Why it matters
Simon Willison experiments with IBM's newly-released Granite 4.1 3B model by testing 21 different GGUF quantized variants against his 'pelican riding a bicycle' SVG benchmark. The post demonstrates how quantization affects output quality at the small model tier, with practical implications for edge deployment. IBM's Granite 4.1 family (Apache 2.0 licensed, 3B/8B/30B sizes) represents a strong open-weights option for enterprise use cases requiring tool-calling and instruction-following.

## Lede (original)
IBM released their Granite 4.1 family of LLMs a few days ago. They're Apache 2.0 licensed and come in 3B, 8B and 30B sizes. Granite 4.1 LLMs: How They're Built by Granite team member Yousaf Shah describes the training process in detail.
