# Anthropic's annualized revenue hits $30 billion

## Why it matters
Anthropic's annualized revenue reached $30B—up from $9B just five months ago—with $1M+ customers doubling from 500 to 1,000 in roughly two months. For context, Salesforce took over 20 years to reach $30B; Anthropic is doing it in 16 months. A new fundraising round targeting $900B valuation may close within weeks. The revenue ramp is structural enterprise adoption, not consumer noise. Also covers the OpenClaw/Claude Code billing controversy.
