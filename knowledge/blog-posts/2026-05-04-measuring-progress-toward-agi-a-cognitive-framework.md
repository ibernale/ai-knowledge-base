# Measuring Progress Toward AGI: A Cognitive Framework

## Why it matters
Google DeepMind publishes 'Measuring Progress Toward AGI: A Cognitive Taxonomy,' a framework for understanding AI systems' cognitive capabilities using insights from cognitive science. They're launching a Kaggle hackathon with $200K prize pool to build relevant evaluations for key cognitive abilities. This addresses the gap in empirical tools for evaluating general intelligence, potentially establishing a more rigorous vocabulary for AGI progress claims.

## Lede (original)
Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) has the potential to accelerate scientific discovery and help solve some of humanity's most pressing problems. But it can be difficult to know how close we are to this key milestone, because there's a lack of empirical tools for evaluating systems' general intelligence. Google DeepMind's new paper, 'Measuring Progress Toward AGI: A Cognitive Taxonomy,' presents a framework for understanding AI systems' cognitive capabilities.
