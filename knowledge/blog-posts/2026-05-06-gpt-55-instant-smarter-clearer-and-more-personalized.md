# GPT-5.5 Instant: smarter, clearer, and more personalized

## Why it matters
OpenAI replaced ChatGPT's default model with GPT-5.5 Instant, claiming 52.5% fewer hallucinations on high-stakes prompts in medicine, law, and finance compared to GPT-5.3 Instant, plus 37.3% fewer inaccurate claims on challenging user-flagged conversations. The model is more concise (30.2% fewer words), reduces gratuitous emoji, and introduces enhanced personalization using past chats, files, and connected Gmail. Benchmark gains include AIME 2025 improvement from 65.4 to 81.2. This is the default model for hundreds of millions of users and signals OpenAI's focus on reliability over capability expansion.

## Lede (original)
We're updating ChatGPT's default model, available to everyone, to be smarter and more accurate, with clearer, more concise answers that feel better tailored to you. Instant is now more dependable, with significant improvements in factuality across the board and the largest gains in domains where accuracy matters most.
