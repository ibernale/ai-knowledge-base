# 2026 Cosmos Lecture: Jack Clark on Human Autonomy

## Why it matters
Jack Clark's upcoming lecture at Oxford titled 'Change is inevitable. Autonomy is not' addresses how people can maintain self-directed lives as AI becomes deeply integrated. As co-founder of Anthropic and author of Import AI, Clark's perspective on human autonomy in an AI-mediated world carries weight. The lecture, part of the Human-Centered AI Lab series, follows Leslie Valiant's 2024 talk on 'Educability.' For policy and alignment researchers, this signals Anthropic's evolving thinking on the societal implications of advanced AI deployment.

## Lede (original)
Jack Clark, co-founder of Anthropic and a Founding Fellow of Cosmos, will deliver the 2026 Cosmos Lecture at the University of Oxford on Wednesday, May 20th. His talk is titled 'Change is inevitable. Autonomy is not' and will cover issues around how we live self-directed lives as AI becomes more integrated with them.
