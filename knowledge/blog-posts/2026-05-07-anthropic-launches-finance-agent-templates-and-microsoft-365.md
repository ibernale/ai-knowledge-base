# Anthropic launches finance agent templates and Microsoft 365 add-ins

## Why it matters
Anthropic launches ten ready-to-run agent templates for financial services work (pitchbooks, KYC screening, month-end close) alongside Claude add-ins for Microsoft 365 (Excel, PowerPoint, Word, Outlook). Claude Opus 4.7 also launches as their most capable model for financial work. The strategy has two tracks: tools for large institutions to configure agents themselves, and a PE-backed joint venture embedding Claude directly into mid-market company operations. Dario Amodei disclosed 80x growth vs. 10x projections.

## Lede (original)
Anthropic is dramatically expanding its footprint in financial services, launching a suite of pre-built AI agents for the world's largest banks and debuting Claude Opus 4.7—its most capable model for financial work yet. The strategy has two tracks: one aimed at the largest institutions, giving them tools to configure and run AI agents themselves; the other aimed at the mid-market, using a new private equity-backed joint venture to embed Claude directly into company operations.
