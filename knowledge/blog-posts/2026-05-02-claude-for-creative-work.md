# Claude for Creative Work

## Why it matters
Anthropic has released nine new connectors integrating Claude with major creative tools including Adobe Creative Cloud, Blender, Autodesk Fusion, Ableton, and Splice. This represents a significant expansion of Claude's agentic capabilities into professional creative workflows. The Blender integration uses MCP, making it accessible to other LLMs and demonstrating Anthropic's commitment to interoperability. Anthropic also joined the Blender Development Fund as a Corporate Patron. The release includes Claude Design for rapid UI prototyping and export to Canva. This signals a strategic move to embed Claude into existing professional toolchains rather than creating competing standalone products.

## Lede (original)
Creative professionals look to technology to expand what's possible in their work. Claude can't replace taste or imagination, but it can open up new ways of working—faster and more ambitious ideation, a more expansive skill set, and the ability for creatives to take on larger-scale projects. Today, with a coalition of partners including Blender, Autodesk, Adobe, Ableton, and Splice, we're releasing a set of connectors—tools that let Claude work alongside the software creative professionals rely on.
